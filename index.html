Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

01
Do I need a doctor's referral to book?

No referral is needed. You can book an assessment with us directly and we'll guide you from there. If your insurer requires a referral, we'll let you know during your first visit.

02
How long is each session?

A first assessment usually takes 45–60 minutes, and follow-up treatment sessions are typically 30–45 minutes depending on your plan.

03
What conditions do you treat?

We treat neck and back pain, shoulder and knee injuries, sciatica, sports injuries, post-surgery rehabilitation, and posture-related issues, among others.

04
How many sessions will I need?

It varies by condition and goals. After your assessment we'll give you a clear, personalized estimate — many patients see meaningful progress within a few sessions.

05
Do you offer home visits?

Yes, home visits are available in selected areas for patients with limited mobility. Reach out on WhatsApp to check availability for your location.

06
Do you accept insurance?

We work with several major insurers and also offer self-pay packages. Contact us with your provider details and we'll confirm your coverage.
